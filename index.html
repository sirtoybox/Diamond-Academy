Welcome to the official Diamond Academy website!

Greetings!

This is the official hub for all things Diamond Academy related! Diamond Academy is a webcomic project about a world where people can buy superpowers! Diamond Academy itself is a school for young heroes looking to sharpen their skills and learn all about powers, as well as learning new abilities and make important connections.

What is the story about?

In a world where people can buy superpowers, a number of up-and-coming youth living in Crystal City train to become superheros at the esteemed superhero training academy, Diamond Academy. Although, along with these superpowers, comes a variety of issues along with it, not limited to just supervillains. A number of protesters, extremists, cults, gangs, and more are the cause of a lot of social turmoil from this new technology. However, Claire Millerite, an up and coming superhero student, plans on reinvigorating a positive outlook on superpowers, while helping as many people as she can along the way!
